Important Notice: C2C is Evolving

January 21, 2025

Recently, we've been increasingly asking ourselves how we should work alone when big corporations might crush our effort of 5 years in 1 year with AI. We believe it's one of the most important product questions of our time.

This page is currently under maintenance and could be shut down after January 21, 2025.

If you need help or more time finding a replacement, email c2c.y3rawat@gmail.com. We're happy to suggest alternatives depending on what exactly you loved about C2C, and we can also grant extensions on a case-by-case basis.

Thank you to everybody who used C2C for jobs. It was a privilege building with you, and we learned a ton from you. We'll miss your feedback and bug reports, but we can't wait to show you what we're up to next.

See you around,
The C2C Team
